# Privacy Policy - Nexus 2048

**Last updated:** March 8, 2026

## Information We Collect
Nexus 2048 does not collect any personal information directly. We use the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob** - displays ads and may collect device information
- **Firebase** - stores leaderboard scores (username and score only)

## Data Stored
- Username and country (only if you choose to join the leaderboard)
- Game scores submitted to the leaderboard

## Ads
This app uses Google AdMob for rewarded ads. Google may collect data as described in their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
